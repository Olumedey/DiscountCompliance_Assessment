COMPLIANT: Denotes whether the discount applied to an account is compliant or non-compliant. Compliant accounts will have value “Y” and non-compliant accounts will have value “N”.,
ACCOUNT_ID: Account number. Unique identifier.,
OPEN_DATE: Date when account was activated.,
SERVICE_PROVINCE: Service province of account.,
DISCOUNT_CODE: Discount code applied.,
DISCOUNT_START_DATE: Start date of discount.,
DISCOUNT_END_DATE: End date of discount.,
AMOUNT: Value of discount; units are in dollars.,
ORDER: Order number that added the discount to account.,
ORDER_DATE: Date of when discount was added to account.,
ORDER_TYPE: Order type.,
SOURCE_SYSTEM: Source billing system where the order was initiated.,
